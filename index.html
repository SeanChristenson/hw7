Instructions

Here are the materials and directions for drawing a square:

Materials

  • Paper
  • Pencil
  • Faith in yourself

Directions

  1. Step 1: Draw a line straight down
  2. Step 2: Draw a line perpendicular to the original line you drew
  3. Step 3: Draw a line going the same direction as the original line went and make it the same length
  4. Step 4: Draw a line connecting the original line and the last line you just drew
